Welcome to John Cooper Clarke's Poems

Here all the poems are presented in different ways. Enjoy!



These poems were encoded using XML to determe some of most conversational elements of Cooper Clarke's poetry. Namely, the poet's use of references, colloquial language, references to people and use of direct language. These make the poems seem to be in conversation with the reader. Each different page shows the words that apply to each different theme, taken from 10 poems that were sourced from John Cooper Clarke's official website. These words are shown in the line that they appear in the text, with the rest of the poem's text cancelled out.
